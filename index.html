Membership Benefits

Monthly Meetings

The garden club holds monthly meetings from February to November. Most months the meeting will involve a speaker on a gardening related topic.
The exception to this is June when the club holds a bring and buy evening, this is an ideal time to get plants at a reduced price.
November is the last meeting of the year and is the presentation evening when the prizes from the annual show are given out.

Trading Shed

The trading shed provides members with the opportunity to purchase compost and fertilisers at a very competitive price, lower than all the local retail outlets.
We buy the fertilisers in bulk but as a member you can buy them in smaller sizes, they are priced by the kg.

Annual Show

The annual show is the highlight of the garden club year and has a variety of classes for vegetables, flowers, flower arranging, wine making and domestic.
With a dedicated childrens section

Trading Shed

The trading shed is open January through to November, every Saturday from 1pm to 3pm (During January 1pm to 2pm).
You need to be a member of the gardening club in order to purchase items from the trading shed.
The purpose of the trading shed is to enable items to be brought in bulk and the cost savings to be shared amongst the members.
